# Multi Brand Marketing Roi Analysis
# Multi-Brand Marketing Campaign Performance and ROI Optimization

## Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze marketing campaign performance across multiple brands and optimize return on investment (ROI) by identifying high-performing channels, customer segments, and budget allocation strategies.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (Data Cleaning and Preparation)
- Tableau (Dashboard Development and Visualization)

## Dataset Description
The dataset consists of marketing campaign data from three brands: Nykaa, Tira, and Purplle.

It includes the following attributes:
- Campaign Type (Email, Influencer, Paid Ads, SEO, Social Media)
- Revenue and Profit
- Conversion Rates
- Customer Segments (College Students, Premium Shoppers, Tier 2 Customers, Working Professionals, Youth)
- Platform-wise Marketing Spend
- Time-based Campaign Performance

Raw datasets from each brand were cleaned, standardized, and merged into a single dataset for unified analysis.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Total Revenue: 8565 Cr
- Total Profit: 5652 Cr
- Average ROI: 269 percent
- Conversion Rate by Campaign Type
- Campaign Profit Over Time
- Campaign-wise Revenue Performance
- Customer Engagement Score
- Customer Acquisition Cost

## Analysis Performed

### Campaign Performance Analysis
- Evaluated revenue generated across different campaign types
- Identified top-performing campaigns based on profit and conversions

### ROI and Profit Trend Analysis
- Analyzed monthly profit trends to identify fluctuations
- Observed a dip in profit during a specific period followed by recovery

### Platform and Channel Analysis
- Identified top platforms based on marketing spend
- Compared performance across Email, Paid Ads, Influencer, SEO, and Social Media

### Customer Segmentation Analysis
- Measured engagement score across different customer segments
- Analyzed acquisition cost distribution across segments

## Business Insights and Recommendations

### Key Insights
- Paid Ads and Influencer campaigns generate the highest revenue
- Conversion rates are relatively consistent (around 20 percent) across all campaign types
- Email and Social Media campaigns drive strong conversions
- Profit trend shows a temporary dip, indicating possible inefficiencies or seasonal impact
- ROI shows a sharp decline at the end, suggesting reduced campaign effectiveness or budget inefficiency
- Nykaa, Tira, and Purplle show different performance trends across campaign types, highlighting the need for brand-specific strategies

### Recommendations
- Reallocate budget towards high-performing campaigns like Paid Ads and Influencer Marketing
- Investigate low-profit periods and optimize campaign strategies during those months
- Focus on improving ROI rather than only increasing revenue
- Optimize underperforming campaigns by improving targeting and content strategy
- Focus on high-engagement customer segments to improve acquisition efficiency

### Business Impact
- Optimizing budget allocation can potentially improve overall ROI by 10 to 15 percent
- Better targeting strategies can reduce customer acquisition cost and increase profitability
